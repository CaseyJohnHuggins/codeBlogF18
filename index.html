Assignment 2

Oh boy this one was a fun one. css is the devil. Fun fact: width does not add up to 100% all the time. Because that would be logical. However, I will grant that fitting an image to its container was surprisingly simple. There's actually a max-width property in css.

Arguably, the coolest thing that happened in the process of completing this week's assignment was the total serendipity when I guessed at the hex value for the orange, and somehow got it right. No way I could do that again in a million years.

The most helpful resource for me this time around was attending lecture. The notes I'd managed to take were really helpful in writing the selectors. Also helpful to me was the question asked on Piazza about the sell-something button. Until I'd read that question, I hadn't even noticed the shadow in the pictures. Other than that, just the occasional sanity checks on stackexchange and w3.

Things I had to google up were just little things like "why isn't overflow:hidden working" and "why doesn't width add to 100%?". The latter of the two searches being the most frustrating revelation of the assignment. Assignment 3

That feeling when you finally get around to hitting a programming assignment and you hit it hard and fast and knock it out in one sitting!

....Okay so that probably wasn't a good idea, but it worked out this time.

This assignment actually worked out really well for me because a lot of it was stuff I'd previously done for classes in the past.

The most helpful resource was, once again, the classroom. I can't emphasize that enough. I might look like I'm falling asleep in the middle of the afternoon lectures, but my notebook is packed with notes. I promise. Seriously. I only googled things as sanity checks this time around, or the occasional mixup like using ".add" instead of ".push" to append to an array. I'm actually starting to come around to Javascript. Before this class, web development was the devil to me.

I had some sweet plans to add some really cool bells and whistles to my code blog this week. That may or may not end up happening. I'll probably end up tweaking it when I'm supposed to be working on my next Data Structues assignment.
